JourneyAV : Processing the Sermon Recording

Created by John Stonecypher, last modified on Jan 19, 2026

At the Journey Church, the AV Team makes a high-quality audio recording of every sermon.

After each Sunday service, the AV Technician processes the recording, following an intricate procedure consisting of three parts.

The pages below provide detailed instructions for each part.

  • Part 1: Convert the WAV file to MP3.

  • Part 2: Archive the WAV.

  • Part 3: Publish the MP3.

To process the sermon recording, start with Part 1.
